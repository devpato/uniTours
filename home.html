ABOUT US

∎

Omega Family Medicine commonly known as Baker Family Medicine is a conveniently located office in the small town of Omega, Georgia. This office was established in 2004 and continues to thrive today. Our office is staffed by Nurse Practitioner who works in conjunction with Dr. Richard Ambrozic. At Omega Family Medicine, we strive to provide exceptional care to patients of all ages. We treat patients as family and want to do what we can to assist you with a healthy and happy lifestyle. Israel Saucedo

APRN, FNP-C, AGACNP-BC
Team Member since 2014

I was born and raised in Chihuahua, Mexico and moved to the United States as an exchange student in my 10th grade of High School. I graduated from Bowen’s Mill Christian Center in Fitzgerald, Georgia in 2001. The medical field has always been a personal interest of mine since I was a child. After High School I attended Abraham Baldwin Agricultural College, where I graduated with my Associate’s Degree in Nursing in 2004 and became a licensed Registered Nurse. I continued my education at Valdosta State University where I completed my Bachelors of Science in Nursing in 2007. I was honored to be invited to be a distinguished member of Sigma Theta Tau Nursing Honor Society. In 2011 I decided to further my education as a Nurse Practitioner through the University of South Alabama. In May 2014 I graduated with my Master’s Degree in Nursing with a focus on Family Nurse Practitioner and Adult-Gerontology Acute Care Nurse Practitioner. I have worked in multiple areas of nursing including Emergency Department, Intensive Care Unit, Nursing Administration and Vascular Nursing. I am a proud husband of eight years to my wife Greter and the father of a 1 yr. old son. I enjoy working with my wonderful staff and together we take pride in providing the most excellent care to our patients and their families.
